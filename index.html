Vancomycin Dosing & Optimisation Support Engine (VDOSE)

📋 Disclaimer & Terms of Use

Please read carefully before proceeding

HSgB Vancomycin Dosing & Optimisation Support Engine (VDOSE) is developed by the Pharmacy Department, Hospital Sungai Buloh to support clinical staff in vancomycin dosing, therapeutic drug monitoring (TDM), and generation of structured clinical note templates at the point of care.

VDOSE is a clinical support tool only and does not replace clinical assessment & professional judgement.

Users Are Required To:

  • Independently verify all calculations before clinical use
  • Interpret outputs within the appropriate patient-specific context
  • Apply professional judgement before acting on any output
⚠️ As VDOSE is newly developed, minor errors may occur. Always verify results before clinical use and report any discrepancies to the development team.
INTERNAL USE ONLY Intended exclusively for HSgB staff. Unauthorised use or distribution is strictly prohibited.
The developers are not responsible for any clinical decisions or outcomes arising from the use or misuse of VDOSE.
